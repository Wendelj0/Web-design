About the Proclamation

The Family: A Proclamation to the World is a declaration made by leaders of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints in 1995. It outlines the divine nature of marriage, family, and the role they play in society.

Read the full Proclamation

Key Principles

  • The divine nature of marriage between a man and a woman.
  • The importance of parents in raising children.
  • The centrality of love, faith, and unity in the family unit.
  • The responsibility of all family members to support and care for one another.
